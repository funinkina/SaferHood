  India Today Web Desk    New Delhi April 12, 2024 UPDATED: April 12, 2024 16:11 IST  Sushil Kumar was interrogated for 4 hours by Delhi Police Crime Branch on Monday (AP Photo)HIGHLIGHTSSagar was beaten up by 20 men inside the Chhatrasal Stadium on May 4Sagar succumbed to his concussion injuries early on May 5Post-mortem report says Sagar was hit by a blunt object on his headThe post-mortem report of Sagar Rana suggests that the wrestler was hit by a blunt object on his head. In the report, the cause of death has been suggested as the stabbing by a blunt object on the head.The Babu Jagjivan Ram Memorial Hospital (BJRM) doctors believe that all the marks found on the body are preceded by death.Sagar, who used to compete in the 97 kg Greco-Roman category, lost his life in a scuffle involving two groups inside the Chhatrasal Stadium parking on May 4.Wrestler Sagar Dhankad was taken to the nearest BJRM hospital at 2: 52 am on May 5 and was then rushed to the Trauma Center, where he died during treatment at 7:15 am.The report suggests that Sagar's body had blue marks in many places. Scars are found from head to knee. His wounds are so deep that the bones are also ruptured.According to the report, the viscera and blood samples have been sealed for examination.Earlier today, Delhi Police's Crime Branch team took Sushil Kumar to the Chhatrasal Stadium in New Delhi as part of its investigation into the murder Sagar Rana. Sushil was taken to the venue as early as 4 am on Tuesday and the crime scene was recreated.The 2-time Olympic medalist was also taken to his Model Town residence for further investigation.Sushil Kumar was questioned for close to 4 hours on Monday by the police after he was remanded to 6 days in police custody.
